Welcome to the EPFL learning experiment!

Can a machine suggest better activities than a teacher? This is the question this experiment (conducted by the CHILI lab at EPFL) is attempting to answer. The idea is to find out if certain types of people are more receptive to certain teaching methods, then use this knowledge to build a system that can provide each student with a learning scenario adapted to their personality.
